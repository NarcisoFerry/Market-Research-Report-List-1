<p><p>The ablation devices market is divided into two main types: thermal and non-thermal ablation. Thermal ablation involves the application of heat to destroy tissues, commonly used in procedures like radiofrequency ablation and laser therapy. In contrast, non-thermal ablation utilizes mechanisms such as cold temperatures (cryotherapy) or chemical agents to achieve tissue destruction. Each type caters to specific medical applications, offering varied approaches for treating conditions like tumors, chronic pain, and vascular diseases, thus enhancing patient outcomes significantly.</p></p>
