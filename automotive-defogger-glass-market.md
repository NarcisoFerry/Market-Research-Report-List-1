Backlite defoggers, located at the rear, help drivers see clearly through the back window, especially in cold or humid conditions. Sidelite defoggers are found in side windows, enhancing visibility and safety. Each type is crucial for maintaining clear vision and enhancing driving safety under various weather conditions.</p></p>
