<p><p>The single-screw extruder gearbox market is segmented into solid shaft and hollow shaft types. Solid shaft gearboxes are characterized by their rigid construction, providing high torque transmission and durability, making them suitable for demanding applications. In contrast, hollow shaft gearboxes feature a central opening, allowing for lightweight designs and easier integration with other components, such as drive systems. The choice between these two types depends on specific application requirements, including space constraints, torque needs, and overall system design.</p></p>
