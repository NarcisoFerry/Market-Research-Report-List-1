<p><p>Electrochemical biosensors are analytical devices that convert biological responses into electrical signals, allowing for the detection and quantification of various analytes. These sensors often utilize biological components such as enzymes, antibodies, or nucleic acids, combined with electrochemical transducers to measure biochemical reactions.
