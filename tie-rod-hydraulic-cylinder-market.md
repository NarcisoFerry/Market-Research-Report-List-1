<p><p>The Tie-Rod Hydraulic Cylinder market includes various types based on functionality. Double acting cylinders provide movement in both directions, offering greater control and force for diverse applications. Multi-stage or tandem cylinders consist of multiple cylinder stages, allowing for higher forces and compact design, ideal for applications needing significant pushing or pulling power in limited spaces.</p></p>
