<p><strong>The Wood-fired Hot Tub Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Heater</li><li>External Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood-fired hot tub market is primarily divided into two types based on heating methods: internal heater and external heater. Internal heater tubs have a heating element located inside the tub, allowing for quick and efficient heating directly in the water. In contrast, external heater models feature a separate wood-burning stove positioned outside the tub, which heats the water through a connected system. Both options provide a unique ambiance and sustainable heating, appealing to eco-conscious consumers seeking a natural relaxation experience.</p></p>
