<p><p>The medical inhaler market comprises various types, including Pressurized Metered Dose Inhalers (PMDIs), Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs), and other inhalation devices. PMDIs deliver a specific dose of medication through a pressurized propellant, providing quick relief, often used for asthma and COPD. DPIs require patients to inhale deeply to disperse the powdered medication, offering a breath-actuated delivery method. Other inhalers may include soft mist inhalers or nebulizers, expanding treatment options based on patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
