<p><p>The Hospital Electronic Medical Records (EMR) market encompasses two primary deployment types: on-premises and cloud-based systems. On-premises EMRs are installed locally within a hospital's infrastructure, providing full control over data security and compliance but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based EMRs are hosted off-site and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, cost-effectiveness, and easier updates. This model enhances collaboration and data sharing while reducing the burden of IT management for healthcare providers.</p></p>
