<p><p>The anti-explosion panel market consists of primarily two types: flat panels and dome panels. Flat panels are designed to provide a robust barrier against blasts, often used in infrastructure where space constraints are minimal. They offer a straightforward installation and effective pressure resistance. In contrast, dome panels feature a curved design that enhances strength and deflects blast waves more efficiently, making them suitable for high-risk environments. Both types serve to mitigate explosion risks in industrial and commercial settings, ensuring safety and structural integrity.</p></p>
