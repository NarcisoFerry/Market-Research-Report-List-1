Double-column disconnectors utilize two columns for added stability and can handle higher current loads. Three-column disconnectors, featuring three vertical columns, are ideal for heavy-duty applications, ensuring robust isolation and improved safety. Each type serves specific operational needs in electrical systems, enhancing reliability and performance.</p></p>
