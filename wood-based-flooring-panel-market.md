<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Wood Based Flooring Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Particleboard (PB)</li><li>Medium density fibreboard (MDF)</li><li>High density fibreboard (HDF)</li><li>Oriented strand board (OSB)</li><li>Plywood</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood-based flooring panel market encompasses various types, each with distinct characteristics. Particleboard (PB) is made from wood particles and resin, offering affordability but lower durability. Medium Density Fibreboard (MDF) is denser and smoother, ideal for decorative applications. High Density Fibreboard (HDF) provides durability and water resistance, making it suitable for high-traffic areas. Oriented Strand Board (OSB) uses strands of wood, known for its strength in construction. Plywood, consisting of layers of wood veneers, combines flexibility and stability, suitable for various flooring needs.</p></p>
