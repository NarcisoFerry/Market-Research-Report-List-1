<p><p>The Forward Collision Avoidance Radar market encompasses three primary technologies: ultrasonic radar, laser radar, and microwave radar. Ultrasonic radar utilizes sound waves to detect obstacles, while laser radar employs light pulses for high-precision distance measurement and target identification. Microwave radar, on the other hand, uses radio waves to detect objects at longer ranges, making it effective in various weather conditions. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of range, accuracy, and environmental adaptability, contributing to enhanced vehicle safety systems.</p></p>
