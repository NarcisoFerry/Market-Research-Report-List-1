<p><ul><li>LV Insulating Shoes</li><li>HV Insulating Shoes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The insulant shoes market is categorized into two primary types: LV (Low Voltage) insulating shoes and HV (High Voltage) insulating shoes. LV insulating shoes are designed for protection against low voltage electrical systems, offering safety for workers in environments where electrical hazards are present but at lower voltage levels. In contrast, HV insulating shoes provide protection against higher voltage applications, ensuring the safety of personnel working in high voltage environments, such as electrical utilities and industrial settings. Both types are critical for ensuring electrical safety compliance.</p></p>
