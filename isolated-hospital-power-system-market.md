<p><strong>The Isolated Hospital Power System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IT system</li><li>TN-S system</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Isolated Hospital Power Systems can be categorized into two main market types: IT systems and TN-S systems. IT systems, or isolated neutral systems, provide high reliability by allowing minimal ground currents, making them suitable for sensitive medical equipment. In contrast, TN-S systems feature a separate grounding path, enhancing safety and reducing the risk of electric shock. Both systems aim to ensure continuous power supply and protect patients and staff, but differ in their grounding methods and operational characteristics.</p></p>
