<p><p>The micro bioreactors market is segmented into two main types: micro-bioreactors and mini-bioreactors. Mini-bioreactors, ranging from 1 to 250ml, facilitate more extensive studies while maintaining manageable sample sizes, often simulating industrial-scale conditions. Both types enhance process efficiency, reduce costs, and accelerate the development of biopharmaceuticals and other biological products.</p></p>
