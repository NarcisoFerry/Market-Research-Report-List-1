<p><p>Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) is a versatile fluoropolymer used in various applications, available in different market types. Granular PTFE is often used in industrial manufacturing, providing excellent mechanical properties. Fine powder PTFE, with smaller particle sizes, enhances processing and uniformity in applications. Dispersion PTFE, suspended in a liquid, is utilized in coatings and sealants for its non-stick properties. Micronized PTFE features ultra-fine particles, improving surface smoothness and performance in high-end applications such as electronics and pharmaceuticals. Each type offers distinct advantages for diverse industries.</p></p>
