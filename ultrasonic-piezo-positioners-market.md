<p><p>Ultrasonic piezo positioners are precision devices that utilize ultrasonic waves in piezoelectric materials to achieve accurate positioning. The market is segmented into linear and rotary types. Linear positioners provide straight motion along a single axis, suitable for applications requiring precise linear movements, such as microscopy and laser alignment. Rotary positioners, on the other hand, enable circular motion, ideal for tasks like optical alignment and automated manufacturing processes. Both types offer high resolution and rapid response, catering to various industrial and research applications.</p></p>
