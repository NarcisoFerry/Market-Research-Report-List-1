<p><strong>The Desalination Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Pumps</li><li>Positive Displacement Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The desalination pumps market comprises two main types: centrifugal pumps and positive displacement pumps. Centrifugal pumps use rotational energy to move water, making them efficient for large-scale desalination operations with high flow rates. They are ideal for processes requiring constant water supply. In contrast, positive displacement pumps operate by trapping a fixed volume of water and then forcing it through the system, offering higher pressure capabilities which are beneficial for reverse osmosis applications, ensuring effective desalination in varied conditions.</p></p>
