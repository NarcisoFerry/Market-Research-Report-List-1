<p><p>The Animal Anti-Rabies Vaccine market serves critical applications in pre-exposure and post-exposure prophylaxis. Pre-exposure prophylaxis targets high-risk groups, such as veterinarians and animal handlers, ensuring immunity before potential exposure to rabies. In contrast, post-exposure prophylaxis is administered after suspected exposure, like animal bites, to prevent the onset of the disease. Both approaches are vital in managing rabies transmission risk, enhancing public health safety, and protecting both animals and humans from this fatal viral infection.</p></p>
