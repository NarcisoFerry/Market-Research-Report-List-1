<p><p>The cardiopulmonary bypass accessory equipment market features two primary types of roller pumps: single roller pumps and double roller pumps. Single roller pumps utilize a single set of rollers to move fluid through tubing, providing a cost-effective solution for basic fluid management. In contrast, double roller pumps incorporate two sets of rollers, offering enhanced flow control and reduced pressure variations, making them ideal for more complex surgical procedures. Both types are essential for maintaining circulation during cardiac surgeries.</p></p>
