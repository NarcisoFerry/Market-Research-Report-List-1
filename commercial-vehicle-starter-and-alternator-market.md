<p><p>The commercial vehicle starter and alternator market can be categorized into two main types: alternators and starter motors. Alternators are devices that generate electrical power to charge the vehicle's battery and supply energy to the electrical system while the engine is running. Starter motors, on the other hand, are responsible for initializing the engine by converting electrical energy from the battery into mechanical energy. Both components are essential for the efficient operation of commercial vehicles, ensuring reliability and performance.</p></p>
