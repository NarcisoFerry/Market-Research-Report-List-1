<p><p>The heat control windshield market features two primary types: heated wire windshields and heated coated windshields. Heated wire windshields utilize embedded conductive wires to generate heat, effectively preventing ice and fog formation, enhancing visibility in cold conditions. In contrast, heated coated windshields employ a transparent conductive coating that offers a sleek appearance and efficient defogging capabilities without compromising aesthetics. Both types cater to automotive needs, providing improved safety and comfort for drivers in varying weather conditions.</p></p>
