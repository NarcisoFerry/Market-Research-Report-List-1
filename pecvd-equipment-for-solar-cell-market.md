<p><p>PECVD (Plasma-Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition) equipment plays a crucial role in the solar cell market, involving two main types: Anode PECVD Systems and Cathode PECVD Systems. Anode PECVD Systems are used to deposit thin films on the anode side, enhancing charge transport and light absorption. In contrast, Cathode PECVD Systems are focused on the deposition on the cathode side, improving electrical conductivity and overall efficiency. Both systems are essential for advancing solar cell technology and optimizing energy conversion.</p></p>
