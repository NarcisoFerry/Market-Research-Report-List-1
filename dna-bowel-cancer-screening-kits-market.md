<p><strong>The DNA Bowel Cancer Screening Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blood Sample</li><li>Fecal Sample</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DNA bowel cancer screening kits primarily utilize two types of samples: blood and fecal. Blood sample kits analyze circulating tumor DNA (ctDNA) to detect cancer-related genetic mutations, offering a non-invasive and convenient screening method. In contrast, fecal sample kits focus on detecting altered DNA or markers in stool samples, which can indicate the presence of colorectal cancer or precursors. Both methods aim to enhance early detection, improving patient outcomes and allowing for timely intervention in bowel cancer cases.</p></p>
