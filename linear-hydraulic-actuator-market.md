<p><ul><li>Single-Acting</li><li>Double-Acting</li><li>Double-Rod</li><li>Rodless</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The linear hydraulic actuator market features several types, each serving distinct applications. Single-acting actuators move in one direction using hydraulic pressure, relying on a spring for return. Double-acting actuators exert force in both directions, providing greater control. Double-rod actuators have rods on both ends, allowing for equal force in either direction. Rodless actuators operate without an extended rod, ideal for space-constrained environments. Other types may include specialized designs tailored for unique industrial needs, enhancing versatility in hydraulic applications.</p></p>
