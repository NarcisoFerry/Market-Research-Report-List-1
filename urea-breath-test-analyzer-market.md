<p><p>The Urea Breath Test Analyzer market is primarily divided into two types: mass spectrometers and infrared spectrometers. Mass spectrometers offer high sensitivity and accuracy by measuring the concentration of isotopes, making them ideal for detecting specific biomarkers in breath samples. Infrared spectrometers, on the other hand, utilize infrared light to identify and quantify gases based on their absorption characteristics, providing a cost-effective and efficient solution for urea detection. Both types cater to the growing demand for non-invasive diagnostic solutions in gastrointestinal health.</p></p>
