<p><p>The Cadmium Zinc Telluride (CZT) crystals market encompasses two primary types: monocrystalline wafers and monocrystalline ingots. Monocrystalline wafers are thin slices of CZT used in semiconductor applications, providing high efficiency and superior performance for devices like detectors in X-ray and gamma-ray imaging. Monocrystalline ingots, on the other hand, are larger blocks of CZT material that can be further processed into wafers. Both forms are essential for advancing technology in various sectors, including medical imaging, nuclear safety, and radiation monitoring.</p></p>
