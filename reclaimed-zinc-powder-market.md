<p><p>The reclaimed zinc powder market is categorized mainly into fine zinc powder and extra coarse zinc powder. Fine zinc powder, typically with a particle size less than 150 microns, is used in applications requiring high reactivity, such as coatings and battery production. Extra coarse zinc powder, with larger particles, is generally utilized in applications where a thicker layer of zinc is needed, like in galvanization. Both types serve vital roles in industries like construction, automotive, and electronics, enhancing corrosion resistance and material performance.</p></p>
