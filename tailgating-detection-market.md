<p><ul><li>Imaging Measurement Tech</li><li>Non-Imaging Tech</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tailgating detection encompasses two primary market types: imaging measurement technology and non-imaging technology. Imaging measurement tech utilizes advanced cameras and sensors to analyze visual data, identifying unauthorized access or tailgating incidents by capturing interactions at entry points. In contrast, non-imaging technology employs methods like RFID, infrared, or thermal sensors to detect and differentiate between individual movements, ensuring compliance with access protocols without direct visual monitoring. Both approaches enhance security in various environments, including corporate offices and secure facilities.</p></p>
