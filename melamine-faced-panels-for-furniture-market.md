<p><p>Melamine-faced panels for furniture are categorized into two primary types: MF-C (Melamine Faced Chipboard) and MF-MDF (Melamine Faced Medium-Density Fiberboard). MF-C utilizes chipboard as its core material, providing a cost-effective and sturdy option for various applications. In contrast, MF-MDF features a denser fiberboard core, offering a smoother surface and better finish, which makes it ideal for high-quality furniture pieces. Both options are popular in the furniture market due to their aesthetic appeal, durability, and ease of maintenance.</p></p>
