<p><p>The marine biofuel oil market primarily consists of two types: distillate fuel oil and residual fuel oil. Distillate fuel oil, including marine diesel, is refined to provide cleaner combustion and lower emissions, making it suitable for newer vessels and regulatory compliance. Residual fuel oil, derived from the heavier fractions of crude oil, is used in larger ships and in areas with less stringent regulations. Both types play critical roles in powering ships while addressing environmental concerns.</p></p>
