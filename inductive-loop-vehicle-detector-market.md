<p><strong>The Inductive Loop Vehicle Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Saw Cut Loop</li><li>Performed Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Inductive Loop Vehicle Detector market comprises two main types: saw cut loops and performed loops. Saw cut loops involve cutting grooves into road surfaces to embed wire for vehicle detection, offering flexibility for installation in various locations. However, this method can be time-consuming and requires significant roadwork. In contrast, performed loops involve pre-constructed loops of wire that are installed during pavement construction. This method is quicker and more efficient, ideal for new roads or large-scale projects, ensuring reliable vehicle detection.</p></p>
