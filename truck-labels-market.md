<p><p>Truck labels are essential for branding, identification, and regulatory compliance. Pressure-sensitive labels adhere with light pressure, offering durability and versatility. Glue-applied labels use adhesives for a strong bond, ideal for rough surfaces. Heat transfer labels are fused onto surfaces using heat, ensuring longevity and resistance to wear. In-mold labels are embedded during manufacturing, providing a seamless, tamper-proof finish. Other markets include specialty labels like RFID or holographic labels, catering to unique needs. Each type serves specific applications, balancing durability, aesthetics, and functionality for trucks.</p></p>
