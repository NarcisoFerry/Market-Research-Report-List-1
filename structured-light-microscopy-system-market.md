<p><p>The Structured Light Microscopy System market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **Projection Structured Light** uses patterns projected onto specimens to capture 3D information, offering high-speed imaging.</p><p>2. **Coded Light** employs coded patterns to enhance depth resolution and improve data collection efficiency, ideal for complex imaging tasks.</p><p>3. **Phase Shift Structured Light** modifies phase patterns to extract precise measurements of surface topography, providing excellent accuracy in 3D imaging. Each type serves distinct applications in biological and materials research, enhancing visualization capabilities.</p></p>
