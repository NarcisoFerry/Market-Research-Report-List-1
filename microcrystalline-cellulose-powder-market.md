<p><p>The microcrystalline cellulose powder market is primarily categorized into two types: refined wood pulp-based and refined cotton-based. Refined wood pulp-based microcrystalline cellulose is derived from the chemical treatment of wood pulp, offering excellent binding and stabilizing properties for various applications in food, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics. On the other hand, refined cotton-based microcrystalline cellulose is sourced from cotton cellulose, providing high purity and a finer texture, making it ideal for specialized applications in dietary supplements and premium products.</p></p>
