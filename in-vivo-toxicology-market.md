<p><p>The in vivo toxicology market encompasses various testing types to assess the safety of substances. Chronic toxicity testing evaluates long-term exposure effects, while sub-chronic toxicity testing focuses on exposures lasting several weeks to months. Sub-acute toxicity testing examines shorter exposures, typically lasting a few days to a week. Acute toxicity testing assesses the immediate effects of a single dose or short-term exposure. Each type is crucial for understanding potential health risks and guiding regulatory decisions for new compounds.</p></p>
