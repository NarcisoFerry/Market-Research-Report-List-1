<p><p>The infrared thermometer market is segmented into three main types: ear, forehead, and multifunction. Ear thermometers measure temperature through the ear canal, offering quick results and accuracy, especially for children. Forehead thermometers use infrared technology to measure surface temperature, ideal for quick screenings without skin contact. Multifunction thermometers combine various measurement methods and features, allowing users to switch between ear, forehead, and even surface temperature readings, providing flexibility for diverse applications in both home and professional settings.</p></p>
