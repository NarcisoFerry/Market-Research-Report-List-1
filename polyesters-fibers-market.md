<p><p>The polyester fibers market encompasses two main types: polyester staple fiber (PSF) and polyester filament yarn (PFY). PSF is produced from short strands, used primarily in textiles for clothing, bedding, and carpets, offering warmth and resilience. PFY, on the other hand, consists of continuous strands, ideal for applications requiring strength and durability, such as upholstery and industrial fabrics. Both types exhibit versatility in production, contributing to the growing demand in the apparel and home furnishings sectors.</p></p>
