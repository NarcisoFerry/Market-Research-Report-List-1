<p><p>The Standalone Medical Vacuum System market is categorized into three main types based on technology. Dry Claw Pump Technology utilizes two rotating claws to create a vacuum without lubrication, ensuring cleanliness and low maintenance. Dry Rotary Vane Technology employs rotating vanes to generate a vacuum using minimal lubrication, suitable for applications requiring high reliability. Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology relies on oil for sealing and cooling, offering efficient vacuum generation but requiring more maintenance due to oil management. Each technology serves specific medical applications.</p></p>
