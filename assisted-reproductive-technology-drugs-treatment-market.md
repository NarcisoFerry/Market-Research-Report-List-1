<p><p>The Assisted Reproductive Technology (ART) drugs market comprises various treatment types that facilitate fertility. GnRH agonists are used to control hormone levels, enabling better ovulation timing. GnRH antagonists serve a similar purpose but with a rapid action to prevent premature ovulation. Gonadotropins are hormones that stimulate ovarian function, while human chorionic gonadotropins (hCG) are crucial for triggering ovulation. Other treatments include supportive medications and supplements that enhance overall reproductive health, improving the success rates of ART procedures.</p></p>
