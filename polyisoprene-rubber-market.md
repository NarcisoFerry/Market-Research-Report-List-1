<p><p>Polyisoprene rubber is categorized into three market types based on the catalyst used in its production: Lithium catalytic, Titanium catalytic, and Rare Earth catalysis. Lithium catalytic polyisoprene offers high elasticity and resilience, making it popular in tire manufacturing. Titanium catalytic polyisoprene is known for its excellent processing capabilities and thermal stability, used in applications like adhesives and sealants. Rare Earth catalysis, while less common, provides unique properties such as improved aging resistance, suitable for specialized applications in automotive and industrial sectors.</p></p>
