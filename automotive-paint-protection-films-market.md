<p><p>The automotive paint protection films market encompasses various types, including PVC, PU, TPU, and others. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) films offer cost-effective protection with moderate flexibility but may lack durability. PU (polyurethane) films provide higher flexibility and clarity, making them suitable for premium applications. TPU (thermoplastic polyurethane) films combine excellent abrasion resistance and self-healing properties, making them a top choice for long-lasting protection. Other films may include specialized composites or treatments tailored for specific automotive needs, enhancing overall vehicle aesthetics and durability.</p></p>
