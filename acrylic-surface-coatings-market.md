<p><p>Acrylic surface coatings are categorized into several types based on their solvents and application methods. </p><p>Waterborne coatings utilize water as the primary solvent, offering low VOC emissions and easy cleanup, making them environmentally friendly. Solventborne coatings contain organic solvents, providing enhanced durability and resistance, suitable for harsher environments. Powder coatings involve applying a dry powder that is cured under heat, resulting in a robust and long-lasting finish. Other market segments may include specialty formulations for niche applications, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
