<p><p>Extracorporeal Shockwave Lithotripsy (ESWL) devices are classified into three main types based on their energy generation methods. Electrohydraulic devices utilize electrical discharges in water to generate shockwaves, effective for hard kidney stones. Piezoelectric devices employ crystalline materials that vibrate to produce focused shockwaves, offering precision in targeting stones. Electromagnetic devices use electromagnetic coils to generate shockwaves, known for rapid energy release and efficient stone fragmentation. Each type serves specific clinical needs, impacting treatment efficacy and patient outcomes.</p></p>
