<p><strong>The Artificial Hip Prosthesis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Total Hip Replacement</li><li>Partial Hip Replacement</li><li>Revision Surgery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The artificial hip prosthesis market is segmented into three main types: total hip replacement, partial hip replacement, and revision surgery. Total hip replacement involves replacing the entire hip joint, commonly used for severe arthritis. Partial hip replacement focuses on replacing only the femoral head, often suited for older patients with specific fractures. Revision surgery is performed to address complications or failures from previous surgeries, ensuring improved function and pain relief. Each type addresses different patient needs and conditions.</p></p>
