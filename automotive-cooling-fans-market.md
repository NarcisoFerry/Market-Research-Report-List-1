<p><strong>The Automotive Cooling Fans Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Fans</li><li>Mechanical Fans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive cooling fans market is primarily divided into two types: electric fans and mechanical fans. Electric fans are driven by an electric motor, offering precise control, efficiency, and the ability to adjust based on engine temperature. They are commonly used in modern vehicles to optimize fuel consumption. Mechanical fans, on the other hand, are connected directly to the engine via a belt, relying on engine speed to operate. They provide reliable cooling but can be less efficient during low-speed operations. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining optimal engine temperatures.</p></p>
