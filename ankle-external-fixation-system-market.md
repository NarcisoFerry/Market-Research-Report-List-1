<p><p>The Ankle External Fixation System is a specialized orthopedic device used to stabilize and align fractures or injuries of the ankle region. It is particularly beneficial in cases of complex fractures or when internal fixation methods are not applicable.</p><p>The Ankle External Fixation System Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by an increasing prevalence of ankle injuries resulting from sports activities, accidents, and the aging population who are prone to fractures.
