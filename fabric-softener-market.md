<p><p>The fabric softener market is primarily segmented into liquid fabric softeners and fabric softener sheets. In contrast, fabric softener sheets are designed for use in dryers, releasing conditioning agents that help soften clothes while also imparting fragrance. Both types aim to improve the washing and drying experience by enhancing fabric feel and reducing wrinkles.</p></p>
