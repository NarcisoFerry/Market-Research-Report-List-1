<p><p>The elliptical leaf spring market is primarily categorized into two types: multi-leaf springs and mono-leaf springs. Multi-leaf springs consist of several layers of steel leaves, providing greater flexibility and load capacity, commonly used in heavy vehicles and trailers. In contrast, mono-leaf springs consist of a single, thicker leaf, offering a more compact design and lighter weight, making them suitable for lighter vehicles and applications where space is limited. Both types are crucial for enhancing vehicle stability and comfort.</p></p>
