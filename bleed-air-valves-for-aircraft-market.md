Dual butterfly motor-operated valves offer efficient airflow management, using rotating discs to minimize pressure loss. Pressure-regulated and shutoff valves maintain optimal pressure levels, safeguarding system integrity. Check valves prevent backflow, ensuring air flows in the correct direction and enhancing overall safety and reliability in aircraft operations.</p></p>
