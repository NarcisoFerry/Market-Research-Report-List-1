<p><ul><li>Half Palm</li><li>Full Palm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The adult orthotic insoles market consists of two primary types: half palm and full palm insoles. Half palm insoles provide support specifically for the arch and heel, offering targeted relief for conditions like plantar fasciitis while allowing for flexibility in the forefoot. In contrast, full palm insoles cover the entire footbed, delivering comprehensive support and cushioning, beneficial for those with broader foot alignment issues or requiring enhanced stability. Both types cater to varying comfort needs, preferences, and foot conditions.</p></p>
