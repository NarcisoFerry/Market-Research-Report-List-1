<p><strong>The Interlaminar Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Interspinous Distraction Devices</li><li>Coflex Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The interlaminar device market primarily comprises interspinous distraction devices and coflex devices. Interspinous distraction devices are designed to relieve spinal canal pressure by creating space between vertebrae, thus alleviating pain associated with conditions like spinal stenosis. The coflex device, on the other hand, is a flexible implant used to provide stabilization while allowing for natural movement of the spine. Both types aim to improve patient outcomes in minimally invasive spine surgeries by enhancing stability and mobility.</p></p>
