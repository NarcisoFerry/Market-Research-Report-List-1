<p><p>The gas separation film market is primarily categorized into three types: hollow fiber, spiral wound, and others. Hollow fiber membranes feature a dense arrangement of porous fibers, optimizing surface area for efficient gas separation. Spiral wound membranes consist of flat sheets rolled into a cylindrical shape, maximizing space and minimizing pressure drops. Other types include coated membranes and composite structures, which offer tailored separation qualities. Each type serves specific industrial applications, enhancing efficiency in gas purification and separation processes.</p></p>
