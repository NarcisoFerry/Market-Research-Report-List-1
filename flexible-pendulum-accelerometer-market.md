<p><strong>The Flexible Pendulum Accelerometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Type</li><li>Piezoresistive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flexible Pendulum Accelerometer market is primarily categorized into two types: piezoelectric and piezoresistive. Piezoelectric accelerometers utilize materials that generate an electric charge in response to mechanical stress, making them ideal for dynamic measurements due to their fast response and wide frequency range. In contrast, piezoresistive accelerometers exploit the change in resistance of materials under stress, enabling precise static and dynamic measurements with better sensitivity at lower frequencies. Both types cater to diverse applications, including automotive, aerospace, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
