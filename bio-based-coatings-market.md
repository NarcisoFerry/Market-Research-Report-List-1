<p><p>The bio-based coatings market encompasses three primary technological categories. Primer surface technologies focus on preparing surfaces with eco-friendly primers that enhance adhesion and protection. Top coat technologies apply a final layer of bio-based coatings, delivering aesthetic finishes and additional protection against environmental factors. Together, these technologies promote sustainable practices in coatings while improving performance and reducing reliance on conventional petrochemical materials.</p></p>
