<p><strong>The MMA Monomer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ACH Method</li><li>Isobutylene Method</li><li>Ethylene Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MMA monomer market is segmented into three primary methods of production: ACH Method, Isobutylene Method, and Ethylene Method. The ACH Method involves the catalytic condensation of acetone cyanohydrin to produce MMA. The Isobutylene Method uses isobutylene and methanol as raw materials, facilitating efficient production at lower costs. The Ethylene Method, on the other hand, relies on the oxidation of propylene to yield MMA. Each method varies in terms of cost, efficiency, and environmental impact, shaping the overall market dynamics.</p></p>
