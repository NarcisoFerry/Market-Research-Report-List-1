<p><p>Non-contact ultrasonic sensors utilize sound waves to detect objects without physical contact. The market is primarily divided into two types: open type and sealed type. Open type sensors are designed for environments where airflow and exposure to ambient conditions occur, making them suitable for general applications. In contrast, sealed type sensors are protected against dust and moisture, ideal for harsher environments. Each type serves specific needs based on application requirements, durability, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
