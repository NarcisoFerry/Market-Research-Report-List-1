<p><strong>The Hydraulic Workover Units Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Workover</li><li>Snubbing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydraulic workover units are essential in the oil and gas industry for well intervention and maintenance tasks. The market is primarily categorized into two types: workover and snubbing. Workover units are employed for various activities such as wellbore cleanouts, repairs, and completions, allowing for efficient management of production wells. In contrast, snubbing units are specifically designed for high-pressure environments, enabling the safe handling of live wells where pressure must be controlled while performing interventions. Both types enhance operational efficiency and safety in well management.</p></p>
