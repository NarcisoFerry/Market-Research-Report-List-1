<p><p>The guitar amplification system market primarily includes two types: guitar amplifier heads and combo guitar amplifiers. Guitar amplifier heads are standalone units that provide amplification and require external speaker cabinets, offering flexibility and customizable sound configurations for professional musicians. In contrast, combo guitar amplifiers integrate the amplifier and speaker in one unit, making them portable and user-friendly, ideal for beginners and smaller gigs. Both types cater to various player needs, from studio recordings to live performances.</p></p>
