<p><p>Dosimeters for Quality Assurance are essential tools used to measure exposure to radiation in various settings. The TLD (Thermoluminescent Dosimeters) employ materials that trap electrons, releasing light upon heating, allowing for accurate dose measurements. OSL (Optically Stimulated Luminescence) Dosimeters utilize similar principles but release light when stimulated by specific wavelengths of light, offering real-time monitoring capabilities. Other dosimeter types may include electronic dosimeters and film badges, each catering to specific radiation measurement needs, enhancing safety and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
