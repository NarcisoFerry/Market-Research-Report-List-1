<p><p>Grease hydraulic pumps primarily fall into two types: piston and positive-displacement. Piston pumps utilize a piston mechanism to generate hydraulic pressure and are known for their efficiency and capability to handle high pressures. Positive-displacement pumps, on the other hand, deliver a fixed volume of grease with each cycle, ensuring consistent flow rates. Both types are essential in various applications, including automotive maintenance and industrial machinery, providing reliable lubrication and contributing to equipment longevity.</p></p>
