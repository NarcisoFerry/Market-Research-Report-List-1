<p><strong>The In-flight Broadband Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>L-band</li><li>Ku-band</li><li>HTS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-flight broadband services utilize different satellite frequency bands to provide internet connectivity. L-band offers low data rates suitable for basic services, often used for voice and light data. Ku-band provides higher data rates and supports streaming and rich media applications, widely adopted in commercial aviation. High-Throughput Satellite (HTS) technology enhances Ku-band capabilities by offering increased bandwidth and efficiency, enabling more users and faster speeds, making it ideal for modern passenger demands on long-haul flights.</p></p>
