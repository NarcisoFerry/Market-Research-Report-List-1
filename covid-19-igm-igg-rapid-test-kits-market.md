<p><p>The COVID-19 IgM IgG Rapid Test Kits market encompasses three primary types: Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT), Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISA), and Neutralization Assays. RDTs provide quick results using a finger prick or blood sample, making them ideal for mass screening. ELISA tests offer higher sensitivity and specificity, suitable for diagnostic laboratories. Neutralization Assays assess the ability of antibodies to block viral infection, crucial for understanding immune responses. Each type plays a distinct role in COVID-19 diagnosis and monitoring.</p></p>
