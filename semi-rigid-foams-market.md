<p><p>The semi-rigid foams market is categorized into two primary types based on elastic modulus: low elastic modulus foams and high elastic modulus foams. Low elastic modulus foams exhibit greater flexibility and are commonly used in applications requiring cushioning and comfort, such as in automotive interiors and furniture. In contrast, high elastic modulus foams provide enhanced structural support and durability, making them suitable for industrial applications, packaging, and construction. Both types offer unique benefits tailored to specific market needs.</p></p>
