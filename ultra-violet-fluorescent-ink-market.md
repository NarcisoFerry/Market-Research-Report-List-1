<p><ul><li>Invisible Ultraviolet Fluorescent Ink</li><li>Visible Ultraviolet Fluorescent Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra-Violet Fluorescent Ink market comprises two main types: Invisible Ultraviolet Fluorescent Ink and Visible Ultraviolet Fluorescent Ink. Invisible UV ink is used in applications where the ink is not visible under normal light but glows under UV light, making it ideal for security printing, such as banknotes and identification cards. In contrast, Visible UV ink is designed to be seen under both regular and UV light, often utilized in marketing materials and signage for added visual appeal and brand enhancement.</p></p>
