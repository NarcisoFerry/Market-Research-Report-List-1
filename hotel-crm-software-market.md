<p><ul><li>Cloud Based</li><li>Premise Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hotel CRM software is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and premise-based. Cloud-based CRM is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and ease of updates. It facilitates real-time collaboration and data access from multiple devices. In contrast, premise-based CRM is installed locally on hotel servers, providing greater control over data security and customization. However, it may require significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance, making it less flexible than cloud solutions.</p></p>
