<p><strong>The Metallic Paint & Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallic paint and coating market is primarily categorized into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based metallic coatings use organic solvents as carriers, offering strong adhesion, durability, and a glossy finish, making them suitable for industrial applications. In contrast, water-based metallic coatings utilize water as a solvent, resulting in lower volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions, easier clean-up, and greater environmental compliance. While water-based options provide moderate durability, advancements have improved their performance for a variety of applications, including residential and commercial use.</p></p>
