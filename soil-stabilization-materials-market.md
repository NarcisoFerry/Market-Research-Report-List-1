<p><p>The soil stabilization materials market includes various types such as polymers, minerals, and stabilizing agents. Mineral-based stabilizers, like lime and cement, increase soil strength and load-bearing capacity. Stabilizing agents, which encompass various chemical additives, help to bind soil particles, reduce plasticity, and improve durability. Together, these materials enhance soil performance, enabling more effective infrastructure development and environmental management.</p></p>
