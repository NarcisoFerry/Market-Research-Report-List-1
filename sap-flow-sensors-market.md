<p><p>Sap flow sensors measure the movement of sap in plants, providing insights into their physiological conditions. The conventional type utilizes heat dissipation methods to gauge sap flow based on temperature differences. Thermopile sensors, on the other hand, employ multiple thermocouples to detect temperature variations, offering enhanced accuracy. Other market types may include advanced technologies like optical or electromagnetic sensors, which use different principles for monitoring sap flow. Each type serves distinct applications based on sensitivity, accuracy, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
