<p><p>The plastic container market is diverse, primarily consisting of five key materials: PET, PP, HDPE, PVC, and LDPE. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) is widely used for beverage bottles, known for its strength and recyclability. PP (Polypropylene) is prized for its versatility and resistance to chemicals, making it ideal for food containers. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) offers durability and is used for milk jugs and detergent bottles. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) serves in rigid applications like pipes, while LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is preferred for flexible packaging and bags. Each type caters to specific end-use needs.</p></p>
