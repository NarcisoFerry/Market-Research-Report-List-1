<p><strong>The Industrial Steam Boilers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fire Tube Or Shell Boilers</li><li>Water Tube Boilers</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial steam boiler market comprises primarily three types: Fire Tube or Shell Boilers, Water Tube Boilers, and Other types. Fire tube boilers contain hot gases within tubes surrounded by water, making them efficient for low-pressure applications. Water tube boilers, conversely, circulate water within tubes heated externally, allowing for higher pressure and capacity. Other types include specialty and hybrid boilers, which cater to specific industrial needs. Each type serves distinct operational requirements, emphasizing efficiency and safety in steam generation.</p></p>
