<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Integrated Touchscreen Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-Cell</li><li>In-Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Integrated Touchscreen Module market is primarily segmented into two types: On-Cell and In-Cell. On-Cell technology incorporates touch sensors onto the display panel itself, enhancing responsiveness while maintaining slim profiles. It offers good visibility and is commonly used in smartphones and tablets. In-Cell technology, on the other hand, integrates the touch functionality directly into the LCD layer, which reduces thickness and enhances durability. This technology is often favored for high-end devices due to its superior display quality and sensitivity.</p></p>
