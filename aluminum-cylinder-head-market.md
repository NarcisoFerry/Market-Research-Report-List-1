<p><p>The Aluminum Cylinder Head market includes Gravity Casting Aluminum Alloy and Low Pressure Casting Aluminum Alloy types. Gravity casting involves pouring molten aluminum into molds using gravity, producing cost-effective, lightweight, and durable cylinder heads with good surface finish. Low-pressure casting uses controlled pressure to fill molds, resulting in higher density, superior mechanical properties, and fewer defects, making it ideal for high-performance applications. Both methods cater to automotive and industrial sectors, balancing cost, performance, and production efficiency.</p></p>
