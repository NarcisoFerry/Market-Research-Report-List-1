<p><strong>The Exhaust Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Dispersive Infrared</li><li>Flame Ionization Detector</li><li>Chemiluminescence Analyser</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Exhaust analyzers are devices used to measure and analyze vehicle emissions. **Non-Dispersive Infrared (NDIR)** analyzers detect gases like CO and CO2 by measuring infrared light absorption. **Flame Ionization Detectors (FID)** measure hydrocarbons by ionizing them in a flame and detecting the resulting current. **Chemiluminescence Analyzers** detect NOx by reacting it with ozone to produce light, which is then measured. **Other** market types include technologies like electrochemical sensors or laser-based systems, offering diverse solutions for emission monitoring across industries.</p></p>
