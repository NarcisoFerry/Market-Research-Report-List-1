<p><p>The dental materials for 3D printing market is segmented into three main types: transparent, translucent, and opaque. Transparent materials are used for applications requiring clarity, such as aligners and splints, providing an aesthetically pleasing finish. Translucent materials mimic the natural appearance of teeth, ideal for creating crowns, bridges, and dentures that blend seamlessly with existing teeth. Opaque materials are primarily utilized in frameworks and certain restorations, offering strength and durability while masking underlying structures, ensuring structural integrity in dental applications.</p></p>
