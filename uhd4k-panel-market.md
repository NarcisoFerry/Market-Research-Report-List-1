<p><p>The UHD/4K panel market is primarily divided into two segments: LED and LCD technologies. LED panels utilize light-emitting diodes for backlighting, enabling enhanced brightness, contrast, and energy efficiency. LCD panels, on the other hand, use liquid crystal displays for image rendering, often requiring backlighting, typically provided by LEDs. Both technologies offer high resolution, but LED panels generally deliver superior color accuracy and viewing angles, while LCD panels are known for cost-effectiveness and broad availability.</p></p>
