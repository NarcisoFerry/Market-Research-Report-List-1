<p><p>The Copper-Nickel Pipes market primarily includes two types: welded and seamless pipes. Welded pipes are made by fusing metal pieces together, offering good dimensional accuracy and being cost-effective for large-scale production. Seamless pipes, on the other hand, are manufactured from a solid piece of metal, providing superior strength and reliability, especially under high pressure and temperature conditions. Both types are crucial in various industries, including marine, oil and gas, and chemical processing, due to their corrosion resistance and durability.</p></p>
