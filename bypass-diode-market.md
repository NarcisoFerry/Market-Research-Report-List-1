<p><p>The bypass diode market is primarily segmented into two types: Through Hole Technology (THT) and Surface Mount Technology (SMT). THT involves the insertion of diodes into pre-drilled holes on a circuit board, providing robust connections ideal for high-power applications. Conversely, SMT mounts components directly onto the surface of a circuit board, allowing for smaller, more compact designs that enhance manufacturing efficiency. Both technologies cater to diverse applications, balancing performance needs with design preferences in the energy and electronics sectors.</p></p>
