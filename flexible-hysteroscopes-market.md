<p><p>The flexible hysteroscopes market comprises primarily two types: flexible video hysteroscopes and flexible fiberoptic hysteroscopes. Flexible video hysteroscopes utilize advanced imaging technology, providing high-resolution video feed for enhanced visualization during uterine examinations and procedures. In contrast, flexible fiberoptic hysteroscopes employ fiberoptic cables to transmit light and images, offering a more traditional approach with varying image quality. Both types cater to different clinical needs and preferences, playing crucial roles in minimally invasive gynecological diagnostics and treatments.</p></p>
