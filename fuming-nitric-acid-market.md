<p><p>The fuming nitric acid market can be segmented into three production methods: Direct Method, Indirect Method, and Hyperazeotropic Distillation. The Direct Method involves the oxidation of ammonia to form nitric oxide, which is then converted into nitric acid. The Indirect Method typically involves the reaction of nitric acid with concentrated sulfuric acid to produce fuming nitric acid. Hyperazeotropic Distillation refines nitric acid to achieve higher purity by distilling it under specific conditions, enhancing efficiency and concentration levels in production.</p></p>
