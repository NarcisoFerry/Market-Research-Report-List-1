<p><p>The Carbon Blacks for Non-rubber Applications market comprises two primary types: Thermal Black and Furnace Black. Thermal Black is produced by the thermal decomposition of hydrocarbons, resulting in a high purity and low structure material, ideal for applications in plastics and coatings. In contrast, Furnace Black is generated through the incomplete combustion of hydrocarbons, leading to a higher surface area and structure, making it suitable for use in inks, pigments, and other industrial applications. Both types enhance performance properties in various products.</p></p>
