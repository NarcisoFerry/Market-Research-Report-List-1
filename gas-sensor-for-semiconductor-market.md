<p><strong>The Gas Sensor for Semiconductor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistive</li><li>Non-resistive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas sensor market for semiconductors is categorized into resistive and non-resistive types. Resistive gas sensors detect changes in resistance caused by gas interactions, making them suitable for measuring various gases like CO, NO2, and CH4. Non-resistive sensors, including capacitive and piezoelectric types, rely on alterations in capacitance or acoustic wave behavior in response to gas presence. Each type serves different applications, with resistive sensors commonly used in consumer electronics and non-resistive sensors found in industrial and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
