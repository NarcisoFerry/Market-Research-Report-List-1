<p><strong>The Conical Twin Screw Extruder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Co-rotating</li><li>Counter-rotating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conical twin screw extruder market is primarily categorized into two types: co-rotating and counter-rotating. Co-rotating extruders feature screws that rotate in the same direction, enhancing material mixing and enabling efficient processing of various polymers. In contrast, counter-rotating extruders have screws that rotate in opposite directions, allowing for better control over material flow and reducing the risk of overheating. Both types are widely used in industries such as plastics and food, tailored to specific processing requirements.</p></p>
