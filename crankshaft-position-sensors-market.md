<p><ul><li>Linear Position Sensor</li><li>Rotary Position Sensor</li><li>Proximity Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The crankshaft position sensor market comprises primarily three types: linear position sensors, rotary position sensors, and proximity sensors. Linear position sensors measure the crankshaft's linear displacement, providing accurate position data for engine timing. Rotary position sensors track the angular position of the crankshaft, crucial for ignition and fuel injection systems. Proximity sensors detect the presence or absence of the crankshaft without direct contact, enhancing durability and reliability. These technologies are essential for optimizing engine performance and efficiency across various automotive applications.</p></p>
