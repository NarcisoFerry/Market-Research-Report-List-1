<p><p>The polyurethane synthetic leather market is primarily divided into two types: dry synthetic and wet synthetic. Dry synthetic leather is produced using a solvent-free process, resulting in a more environmentally friendly product with applications in fashion and upholstery, known for its durability and wide range of textures. In contrast, wet synthetic leather involves a coating process that requires solvents, leading to a softer and more flexible feel, commonly used in automotive interiors and high-end products. Each type caters to distinct consumer preferences and industry needs.</p></p>
