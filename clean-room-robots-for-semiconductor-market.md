<p><p>Clean room robots for the semiconductor market are specialized automated systems designed to operate in ultra-clean environments, critical for manufacturing processes. Vacuum robots maintain a controlled atmosphere by minimizing airborne contaminants, ensuring high-precision tasks while adhering to stringent cleanliness standards. In contrast, atmospheric robots function in conditions where ambient air is utilized, often found in less sensitive processes. Both types focus on enhancing efficiency, reducing human intervention, and ensuring product integrity in semiconductor fabrication and assembly.</p></p>
