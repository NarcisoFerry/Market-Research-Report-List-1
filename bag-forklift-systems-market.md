<p><p>Bag forklift systems are categorized into electric and non-electric markets based on their power source and operational efficiency. Electric bag forklifts are powered by batteries, offering advantages like reduced emissions, quieter operation, and lower operational costs, making them suitable for indoor use and environmentally-conscious businesses. In contrast, non-electric bag forklifts are often maneuvered manually or powered by internal combustion engines, providing portability and ease of use in outdoor settings, making them ideal for rugged conditions and heavy-duty tasks.</p></p>
