<p><ul><li>Single Acting Electro-Pneumatic Valve Positioners</li><li>Double Acting Electro-Pneumatic Valve Positioners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Analog Valve Positioners market consists of two main types: Single Acting and Double Acting Electro-Pneumatic Valve Positioners. Single Acting Valve Positioners utilize a spring mechanism to return the valve to its default position when not engaged, offering a simpler control system. In contrast, Double Acting Valve Positioners use both pneumatic pressure and spring action to control the valve position with greater accuracy and response. This dual mechanism enhances the operational efficiency and precision of valve control in industrial systems.</p></p>
