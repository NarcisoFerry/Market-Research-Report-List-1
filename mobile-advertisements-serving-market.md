<p><p>Mobile advertisement serving market consists of two main types: first-party ad servers and third-party ad servers. First-party ad servers manage and deliver ads directly from the advertiser's own platform, ensuring control over ad content and audience targeting. In contrast, third-party ad servers operate independently, acting as intermediaries that collect data from multiple sources to optimize ad delivery across various platforms and networks. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the effectiveness of mobile advertising campaigns and reaching targeted consumers effectively.</p></p>
