<p><p>The elastic adhesives and sealants market includes three primary types: polyurethane, silicone, and SMP (Silyl Modified Polymer). Polyurethane adhesives offer strong bonding and flexibility, making them suitable for a wide range of applications, including construction and automotive. Silicone sealants are known for their high-temperature resistance and water repellency, ideal for sealing joints in bathrooms, kitchens, and outdoor structures. SMP combines the benefits of both polyurethane and silicone, providing excellent adhesion and flexibility while being environmentally friendly, making it versatile across various industries.</p></p>
