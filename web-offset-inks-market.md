<p><ul><li>Coldset Inks</li><li>Heatset Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The web offset inks market is primarily categorized into coldset and heatset inks. Coldset inks are used for uncoated paper, allowing quick drying without heat, making them ideal for newspapers and low-cost publications. Conversely, heatset inks require heating during the drying process, making them suitable for high-quality, glossy printed materials like magazines and brochures. Both types offer distinct advantages based on printing requirements, paper types, and desired print quality, catering to various segments of the printing industry.</p></p>
