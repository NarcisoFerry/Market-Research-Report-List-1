<p><p>PU resins are essential in the production of artificial leather, available primarily in two forms: dry-process and wet-process synthetic leather. Dry-process synthetic leather utilizes a solvent-free method, producing a more sustainable and environmentally friendly product. In contrast, wet-process synthetic leather involves the application of PU resins through a liquid process, typically resulting in enhanced flexibility and softness. Both methods cater to diverse market preferences, with applications ranging from apparel to upholstery and automotive industries.</p></p>
