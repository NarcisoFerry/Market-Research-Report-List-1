<p><p>Concrete mixer lorries are categorized into Wet Type, Dry Type, and Other markets based on their operational methods. Wet Type mixers transport pre-mixed concrete, ensuring consistency and reducing on-site mixing time. Dry Type mixers carry dry ingredients and water separately, mixing them at the construction site, offering flexibility and longer transit times. The Other market includes specialized mixers like volumetric or mobile mixers, catering to unique project needs. Each type serves distinct construction requirements, balancing efficiency, convenience, and adaptability.</p></p>
