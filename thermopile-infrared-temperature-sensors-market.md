<p><p>Thermopile infrared temperature sensors are categorized into two main types: Through Hole Type and SMD (Surface Mount Device) Type. Through Hole Type sensors feature leads that are inserted into holes on a printed circuit board, providing robust physical connectivity, making them suitable for high-demand applications. In contrast, SMD Type sensors are designed for surface mounting, allowing for compact designs and automated assembly processes. Both types are utilized in various industries for non-contact temperature measurement, optimizing efficiency and accuracy in thermal sensing.</p></p>
