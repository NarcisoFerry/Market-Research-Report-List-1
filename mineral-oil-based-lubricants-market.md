<p><p>The mineral-oil-based lubricants market can be categorized into three types: fluid, semi-fluid, and solid. Fluid lubricants, such as oils and greases, facilitate easy movement between surfaces, reducing friction in machinery. Semi-fluid lubricants, often in the form of thickened oils, provide enhanced adhesion and protection in varying temperatures. Solid lubricants, like powders or pastes, offer a resilient solution for extreme conditions, minimizing wear and tear on contact surfaces. Each type serves distinct applications across various industries.</p></p>
