<p><strong>The Reverse Osmosis Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cellulose-Based Membranes</li><li>Thin Film Composite Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reverse osmosis membrane market is primarily categorized into two types: cellulose-based membranes and thin film composite membranes. Cellulose-based membranes are made from natural materials, offering good performance in certain applications but are less durable and have lower rejection rates compared to newer types. Thin film composite membranes, on the other hand, consist of layered synthetic materials, providing higher efficiency, better salt rejection, and greater stability. These attributes make them increasingly popular in various water purification and desalination applications.</p></p>
