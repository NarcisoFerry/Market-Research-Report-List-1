<p><p>The lime for the iron and steel market is primarily divided into two main types: quicklime and hydrated lime. Quicklime is produced by calcining limestone and is crucial in steelmaking for removing impurities and improving processing efficiency. Hydrated lime, obtained by adding water to quicklime, is used for refining molten steel and ensures better control over chemical reactions. Both types play vital roles in enhancing product quality and optimizing production processes in the iron and steel industry.</p></p>
