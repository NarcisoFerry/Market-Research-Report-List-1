<p><p>The Quick Lime market consists primarily of two types: High Calcium Quicklime and Dolomitic Quicklime. High Calcium Quicklime, primarily composed of calcium oxide, is used in applications such as steel manufacturing, water treatment, and construction. Dolomitic Quicklime, containing both calcium and magnesium oxides, is favored for its higher ability to neutralize acidity and is often utilized in environmental applications, agriculture, and the pulp and paper industry. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, driving its market demand.</p></p>
