<p><p>The graphene composites market encompasses various types, including graphene oxide, graphene film, and graphene nanoplatelets. Graphene oxide, a modified form of graphene, is valued for its dispersibility in solvents and applications in batteries and coatings. Graphene film, known for its thinness and conductivity, is used in electronics and sensors. Graphene nanoplatelets, small graphene flakes, enhance the mechanical properties of materials and find applications in polymers and composites, boosting strength and thermal conductivity across diverse industries.</p></p>
