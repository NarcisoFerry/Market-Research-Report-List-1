<p><p>The specialty fertilizers market comprises various products tailored to enhance crop yield and quality. Ammonium Nitrate and UAN (30%) provide nitrogen, crucial for plant growth. Phosphoric Acid and Monoammonium Phosphate (MAP) supply essential phosphorus, while Monopotassium Phosphate (MKP) offers both potassium and phosphorus. Potassium Sulfate and Potassium Chloride enhance potassium levels, vital for flowering and fruiting. Potassium Nitrate combines potassium and nitrogen, and Calcium Nitrate (CN) contributes both calcium and nitrogen. NPKs are balanced fertilizers delivering nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium for comprehensive plant nutrition.</p></p>
