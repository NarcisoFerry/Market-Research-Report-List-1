<p><p>Chelated agricultural micronutrients are essential nutrients that are bound to organic molecules, enhancing their availability and absorption by plants. These micronutrients, including zinc, iron, manganese, and copper, play crucial roles in various physiological processes within plants, promoting healthy growth and improving crop quality. The chelation process protects these nutrients from soil interactions that can render them unavailable, making them more effective in agricultural applications.</p><p>The Chelated Agricultural Micronutrients Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.1% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by an increasing demand for high-yield crops, the need for sustainable agriculture practices, and the adoption of precision farming techniques. Additionally, the rising awareness among farmers about the benefits of micronutrient applications is propelling market expansion. </p><p>As environmental concerns grow, there is a trend towards using bio-based chelates, which are perceived as more sustainable alternatives compared to synthetic options.
