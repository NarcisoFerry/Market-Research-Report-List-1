<p><p>The PCB membrane switch market comprises primarily two materials: polyester (PET) and polycarbonate (PC). PET switches are notable for their durability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for various applications. They typically offer better graphic quality and are resistant to chemicals. Conversely, PC switches are favored for their high impact resistance and superior electrical properties, making them ideal for rugged environments. Both types cater to diverse industries, including consumer electronics, automotive, and medical devices, reflecting varying needs in performance and aesthetics.</p></p>
