<p><p>The hybrid vehicle transmission market is primarily categorized into single gear transmission and multi gear transmission types. Single gear transmission systems use a fixed gear ratio, providing simplicity and efficiency, suitable for low-speed applications. In contrast, multi gear transmission systems offer multiple gear ratios, enabling better torque management and fuel efficiency across various driving conditions. This flexibility enhances performance, making multi gear systems more popular in modern hybrid vehicles, where power output and adaptability are crucial for varied driving environments.</p></p>
