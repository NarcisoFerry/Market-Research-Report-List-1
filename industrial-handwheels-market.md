<p><p>The industrial handwheels market features three primary types: spoked handwheels, solid handwheels, and arm handwheels. Spoked handwheels, equipped with multiple spokes, offer lightweight construction and easy grip for manual operations. Solid handwheels provide durability and strength, suitable for high-torque applications. Arm handwheels, featuring a protruding arm or lever, enhance torque application and operator control, particularly in heavy machinery. Each type is designed to optimize performance and efficiency in various industrial settings, fulfilling diverse operational needs.</p></p>
