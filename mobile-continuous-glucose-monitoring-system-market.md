<p><p>The Mobile Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) System market is categorized into two main types: invasive and non-invasive. Invasive CGM systems involve the insertion of a sensor under the skin to provide real-time glucose readings, offering accurate and reliable data for diabetes management. Non-invasive (exfoliation) systems, on the other hand, monitor glucose levels without skin penetration, utilizing methods like light or electromagnetic sensors. This category is gaining traction due to its comfort and ease of use, appealing to consumers seeking less invasive monitoring options.</p></p>
