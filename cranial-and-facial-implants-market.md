<p><p>The cranial and facial implants market comprises three primary types: Polymethyl Methacrylate (PMMA), Porous Polyethylene, and Titanium. Porous Polyethylene, known for its bio-integration capabilities, provides structural support while facilitating tissue growth. Titanium implants offer high strength and biocompatibility, making them ideal for long-term applications in facial reconstruction. Each material serves unique medical needs in cranial and facial repair.</p></p>
