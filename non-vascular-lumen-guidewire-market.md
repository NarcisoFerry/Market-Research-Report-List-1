<p><p>The non-vascular lumen guidewire market comprises various types designed for specific medical applications. Straight guidewires are primarily used for navigating through straight passages, providing a direct path for other instruments during procedures. Elbow guidewires, on the other hand, feature a curved design that enables maneuvering around bends and obstacles within the lumen, facilitating access to difficult-to-reach areas. Both types are essential tools in minimally invasive procedures, enhancing precision and effectiveness in various clinical settings.</p></p>
