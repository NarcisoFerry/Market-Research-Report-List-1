<p><p>Print On Demand (POD) software serves two primary market types: integrated and independent. Integrated POD solutions are embedded within e-commerce platforms, allowing users to manage printing, inventory, and fulfillment seamlessly alongside their online stores. In contrast, independent POD software operates as standalone systems, giving users more flexibility in selecting their e-commerce and printing partners. Both types cater to different user needs; integrated systems streamline processes for convenience, while independent solutions offer greater customization and control over the printing experience.</p></p>
