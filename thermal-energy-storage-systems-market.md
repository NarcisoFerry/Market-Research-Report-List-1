<p><p>Thermal energy storage systems encompass various technologies for efficiently storing energy. Solar energy storage captures excess solar heat for later use. Molten-salt technology utilizes molten salts to store thermal energy, enabling power generation even when solar output is low. Hot silicon technology involves heating silicon to store and release energy. Pumped-heat electricity storage compresses and stores heat, while cryogenic energy storage uses cryogenic liquids to store energy by cooling gases. Each technology enhances energy availability and grid stability.</p></p>
