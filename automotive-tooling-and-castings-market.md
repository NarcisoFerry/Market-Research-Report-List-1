<p><p>Automotive tooling and castings are critical components in the manufacturing of vehicles, encompassing the design, development, and production of molds, dies, and cast parts used in automotive assembly. These processes are essential for producing lightweight, durable, and complex automotive parts, such as engine blocks, transmission housings, and structural components.</p><p>The Automotive Tooling and Castings Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.9% during the forecast period. These factors are shaping the future of the automotive tooling and castings industry.</p></p>
