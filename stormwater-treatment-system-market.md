<p><p>The Stormwater Treatment System market comprises various types aimed at managing runoff effectively. Screening systems remove large debris, while separation systems utilize physical processes to isolate pollutants based on density differences. Filtration systems use media to capture fine particles, and infiltration systems allow stormwater to percolate into the ground, replenishing groundwater. Retention systems hold water temporarily to facilitate sedimentation and pollutant removal. Additionally, other market solutions include advanced technologies like biofiltration and constructed wetlands for enhanced treatment.</p></p>
