<p><p>Cyclopentadiene (CPD) is a highly reactive organic compound with the chemical formula C5H6. Its derivatives, such as dicyclopentadiene (DCPD), are widely utilized in the manufacturing of adhesives, coatings, and flame retardants. <p><p>The Cyclopentadiene (CPD) market serves various industries through its derivatives. Unsaturated polyester resins, incorporating CPD, are vital in composites for construction and automotive sectors. EPDM elastomers, enhanced with CPD, provide durability in automotive seals and roofing. Poly nDCPD, a polymerized form, offers high-impact resistance in automotive parts.
