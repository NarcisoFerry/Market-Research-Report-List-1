<p><p>The Air Core Fixed Inductors market includes two primary types: multi-layer type and wire wound type. Multi-layer inductors consist of multiple stacked layers of conductive materials, offering compact designs and enhanced performance at high frequencies. In contrast, wire wound inductors are made by winding conductive wire around a core, providing high inductance values and stability. Both types cater to various applications in electronics, including power supplies and communication devices, each presenting distinct advantages in terms of size, efficiency, and performance.</p></p>
