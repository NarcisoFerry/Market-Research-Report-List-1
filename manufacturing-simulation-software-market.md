<p><p>Manufacturing simulation software is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based manufacturing simulation software allows users to access simulations via the internet, providing flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs. It facilitates collaboration among distributed teams and enables regular updates. In contrast, on-premise manufacturing simulation software is installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control over data security and customization. This option is preferred by organizations with stringent regulatory requirements or existing infrastructure.</p></p>
