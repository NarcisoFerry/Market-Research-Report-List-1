<p><p>The Intermittent Pneumatic Compression (IPC) System market consists of two primary types: IPC garments and IPC devices. IPC garments are specially designed clothing equipped with inflatable chambers that apply rhythmic pressure to enhance blood circulation and reduce swelling, commonly used in rehabilitation and post-operative care. IPC devices, on the other hand, are standalone machines that provide controlled pneumatic compression treatment through attached cuffs or sleeves. Both types aim to improve venous flow and prevent complications such as deep vein thrombosis.</p></p>
