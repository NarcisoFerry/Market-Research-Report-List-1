<p><strong>The Cardioverter Defibrillator Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillator</li><li>External Cardioverter Defibrillator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cardioverter Defibrillator Devices market comprises two main types: Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) and External Cardioverter Defibrillators. ICDs are surgically placed inside the body to monitor heart rhythms and deliver shocks when necessary, providing long-term management for patients at risk of sudden cardiac arrest. In contrast, External Cardioverter Defibrillators are portable devices used in emergencies to restore normal heart rhythm using electric shocks, typically found in hospitals, ambulances, and public spaces for immediate response to cardiac events.</p></p>
