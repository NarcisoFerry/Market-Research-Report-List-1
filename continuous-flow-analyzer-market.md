<p><p>The Continuous Flow Analyzer (CFA) market consists of two primary types: Segmented Flow Analyzers and Injected Flow Analyzers. Segmented Flow Analyzers utilize discrete segments of the sample driven by a continuous carrier stream, allowing for multiple analyses without sample loss. In contrast, Injected Flow Analyzers introduce samples into a continuous stream, facilitating real-time measurements. Both technologies enhance efficiency and accuracy in various applications, including environmental monitoring, clinical diagnostics, and industrial processes, catering to diverse analytical needs in continuous measurements.</p></p>
