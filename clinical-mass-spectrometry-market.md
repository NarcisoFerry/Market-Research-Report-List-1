<p><p>The clinical mass spectrometry market comprises various techniques, including LC-MS/MS and MALDI-TOF, among others. LC-MS/MS, or liquid chromatography-tandem mass spectrometry, is widely used for analyzing complex biological samples due to its sensitivity and specificity, making it ideal for pharmaceuticals and biomonitoring. MALDI-TOF, or matrix-assisted laser desorption/ionization time-of-flight mass spectrometry, excels in rapid microbial identification and biomolecular analysis. Other technologies include various hybrid systems, each offering unique advantages for diverse clinical applications, enhancing diagnostic capabilities in healthcare.</p></p>
