<p><strong>The Rebar Coupler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tapered Thread Type</li><li>Parallel Thread Type</li><li>MBT Type</li><li>Grout Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rebar coupler market encompasses various types, including Tapered Thread, Parallel Thread, MBT (Mechanical Bar Connection), and Grout types. Tapered Thread couplers feature a conical design for improved alignment and load transfer. Parallel Thread couplers utilize straight threads for straightforward assembly and strong connections. MBT types provide mechanical interlocking for robust reinforcement. Grout types, on the other hand, use a cement-based grout to bond rebars, ensuring smooth integration and enhanced durability in construction applications. Each type serves specific project needs and requirements.</p></p>
