<p><p>The Sterilization Indicator Tape market includes various types designed for different sterilization methods. Steam Indicator Tapes change color when exposed to steam, indicating successful sterilization. Hydrogen Peroxide Tapes react to low-temperature hydrogen peroxide gas, confirming the effectiveness of the process. EO Sterilization Indicator Tapes are used for ethylene oxide sterilization, providing visual assurance of exposure. Dry Heat Tapes are tailored for dry heat sterilization methods, ensuring items have undergone appropriate sterilization conditions. Each type enhances safety and efficacy in medical settings.</p></p>
