<p><ul><li>Transmission Device</li><li>Rotary Device</li><li>Feed & Discharge Hopper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Sintering Annular Cooler Equipment market encompasses various types, including transmission devices, rotary devices, and feed and discharge hoppers. Transmission devices facilitate the movement of cooled sinter within the cooling system, ensuring efficient operation. Rotary devices enhance airflow and material distribution, promoting uniform cooling. Feed hoppers are responsible for introducing material into the cooling system, while discharge hoppers manage the outflow of cooled sinter, enabling streamlined processing and improving overall productivity in the sintering process.</p></p>
