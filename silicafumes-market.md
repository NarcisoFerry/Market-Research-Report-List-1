<p><p>The silica fume market comprises three main types: densified, semi-densified, and undensified silica fume. Densified silica fume features a compact form, enhancing flowability and reducing dust, making it ideal for concrete applications. Semi-densified silica fume offers a balance between flowability and handling, suitable for various construction needs. Undensified silica fume is less processed, providing high reactivity but requiring careful handling due to its fine particles and dust generation. Each type serves specific industrial applications based on performance requirements.</p></p>
