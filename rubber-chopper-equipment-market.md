Medium rotation speed models strike a balance between efficiency and product integrity, suitable for general rubber recycling tasks. High rotation speed choppers offer maximum throughput, ideal for high-volume operations but may generate more heat, requiring effective cooling systems to maintain material quality.</p></p>
