<p><p>In-Mold Label (IML) Film is a versatile solution utilized in various molding processes to enhance product packaging aesthetics and functionality. In blow molding, IML film is applied during the formation of hollow objects like bottles. In injection molding, the film is integrated during the creation of solid plastic items, ensuring labels are embedded in the surface, enhancing durability. Thermoforming employs IML film for shaping materials into lightweight, custom containers. Each method leverages IML for improved branding and efficient production.</p></p>
