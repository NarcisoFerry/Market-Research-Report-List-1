<p><strong>The ECG Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monitoring Equipment</li><li>Holter Monitoring Systems</li><li>Event Monitoring Systems</li><li>Stress Testing Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ECG equipment market comprises several key types, including monitoring equipment, Holter monitoring systems, event monitoring systems, and stress testing systems. Monitoring equipment is used for continuous heart rhythm tracking, while Holter monitors provide extended wear for 24-48 hours to capture irregularities. Event monitoring systems allow patients to record heart activity during symptoms, typically over a longer duration. Stress testing systems assess cardiac response during physical exertion, helping diagnose conditions like coronary artery disease and guiding treatment decisions.</p></p>
