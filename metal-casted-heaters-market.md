<p><p>The metal casted heaters market comprises three primary types: aluminum, copper, and cast iron. Aluminum heaters are lightweight and provide rapid heat conduction, making them ideal for applications requiring quick heating. Copper heaters offer excellent thermal conductivity, ensuring efficient heat distribution, often used in specialized industrial settings. Cast iron heaters are known for their durability and heat retention, suitable for long-lasting applications like residential heating systems. Each type serves distinct needs based on thermal efficiency, weight, and durability.</p></p>
