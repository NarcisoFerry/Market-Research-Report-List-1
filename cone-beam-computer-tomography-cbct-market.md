<p><p>Cone Beam Computer Tomography (CBCT) is an advanced imaging technique that provides three-dimensional (3D) visualization of dental and maxillofacial structures. CBCT is widely utilized in dentistry, orthodontics, and various surgical applications, enabling precise diagnostics and treatment planning.</p><p>The market for CBCT is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for dental procedures, advancements in imaging technology, and a rising focus on minimally invasive treatments.
