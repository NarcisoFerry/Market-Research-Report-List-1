<p><p>Evaporation equipment for whey products includes techniques like evaporator drying and straight-through spray drying. Evaporator drying involves concentrating whey by removing water through heat, enhancing nutritional value and shelf life. Straight-through spray drying transforms liquid whey into powder by spraying it into hot air, quickly evaporating moisture and preserving desirable properties. Both methods facilitate efficient production and storage of whey protein, catering to demands in food, nutrition, and pharmaceuticals, while ensuring product quality and consistency.</p></p>
